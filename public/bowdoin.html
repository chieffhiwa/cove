HOW IT WORKS

It starts with the right question.

Your LinkedIn profile is a snapshot. Your résumé is a highlight reel. Neither asks what you actually want. Cove starts there — and builds from it.

01 — THE CONVERSATION
We ask the right questions

You answer questions. Real ones — about where you are, what you want, what's holding you back. Your profile builds as you go.

The average job search takes 5 months. Most of that time is spent on the wrong things. Cove starts by getting the right ones clear.
02 — THE MIRROR
You see yourself in others

Cove surfaces Bowdoin alumni who've walked your path. You see who they are, where they landed, and how they got there. Warm intros beat cold applications every time.

70% reply rate on warm intros vs. 0.3% cold. Job offers come from people, not job boards.
03 — THE COACH
A human in your corner

A real coach holds the thread — reading between the lines, knowing when to push, when to listen. The AI brings the questions. The coach brings the judgment.

Parents are paying $15,000+ for private career coaches. Cove is built to make that unfair advantage available to everyone.
