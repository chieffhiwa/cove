HOW IT WORKS

Three moves. All of them matter.

Cove doesn't give you a list of tips. It gives you a framework, a coach, and a tool to build the relationships that actually move the needle.

01 — KNOW YOURSELF
The Bravery / Curiosity Matrix

Before strategy, self-awareness. You place yourself on two axes: Brave vs. Fearful, Curious vs. Judgmental. No wrong answers — just an honest read on where you're starting from.

Research says: self-awareness is the #1 predictor of career satisfaction. Most people skip this step entirely.
02 — BUILD THE LIST
Your List of 100

Name 100 people who are doing something you want to do. Bowdoin alumni, professors, coaches, peers. Warm beats cold every time — and your network is bigger than you think.

70% reply rate on warm intros vs. 0.3% cold. The network works when you work it.
03 — TALK IT OUT
Your AI Career Coach

An honest, calm coach available whenever you need it. One good question at a time — until you see what you couldn't see before.

Gen Z graduates report career anxiety at 3× the rate of previous generations. Having a coach isn't a luxury. It's a necessity.
